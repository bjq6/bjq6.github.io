Projects

Brendan is a strong proponent of a “make-ocracy” - a word of his own creation where you can choose what you want to make and make it. It is an idea that people shouldn’t be held back from being able to make positive change a reality.

CUAir

CUAir is an interdisciplinary project team combining aspects of computer science, engineering, and business. The team aims to provide students from all majors at Cornell with an opportunity to learn about unmanned air systems in a hands-on setting.

Shaper Tools

Shaper is a human-involved robotics company that integrates computers with handheld power tools to simplify and improve the process of making.

Brendan is the Team Lead of CUAir, Cornell University Unmanned Air Systems Team. He manages a team of over 50 engineering students. Each year, our goal is to build a custom aircraft capable of fully autonomous flight, aerial surveillance, obstacle avoidance, and payload delivery to compete in the AUVSI SUAS competition.

Electric Skateboard

One of many projects "just for fun", Brendan made the electric skateboard to help him get around the hills of Ithaca in a split. Using a Turnigy Aerodrive SK3 - 5055-280kv Brushless Outrunner Motor, an 80A RC car Electronic Speed Controller, and an 8-to-3 gear ratio, the skateboard is able to get to 30 km/h!

  • Website: brendanquinn.me
  • Dates: February 2017 - Present
  • Location: Cornell University - Ithaca, NY

Shaper Tools

At Shaper, Brendan was able to help bring autocorrect to the everyone’s hands with the first handheld CNC machine. Working with fellow maker and former Kessler Fellow Jeremy Blum ‘12, M.Eng ‘13, the Shaper team has built a tool to help people shape wood, plastics, composites, and soft metals from stock and standard to original and extraordinary. Brendan worked as an Embedded Systems engineer for Shaper, building out the Real-Time Operating System and peripheral drivers that will run their tool.

  • Website: shapertools.com
  • Dates: June 2017 - August 2017
  • Location: Shaper Tools - San Francisco, CA
